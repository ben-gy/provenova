## How to sign

Signing is electronic and takes one comment. When you open your first pull request, the CLA
bot will ask you to read this document and reply with exactly:

> I have read the CLA and I hereby sign the CLA

Your GitHub username and a timestamp are recorded in the Project's signature file
(`.github/signatures/cla.json` on the `cla-signatures` branch). One signature covers all your
subsequent Contributions to the Project.
